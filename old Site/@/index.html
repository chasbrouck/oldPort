Though At Work - Website 2016

Directly quoting the Thought At Work website - "Thought At Work is a three-day, student-run, student-focused design conference that takes place every October at Rochester Institute of Technology."

For the 2016 conference, I worked along side Jake Frank, Cole Johnson and Amy Pham to develop the conference website.

Git Hub: https://github.com/ThoughtAtWork/Website

Live Site: http://thoughtatwork.cias.rit.edu/

My Role

The 2016 Thought at Work website utilizes a bootstrap framework to assist in responsive development. However, the website design contained many elements that required specific attention on tablet/mobile views. It was my job to develop these custom interfaces/designs for seamless responsive viewing. Additionally, I assisted in the development of various desktop elements. Below are a few examples.
